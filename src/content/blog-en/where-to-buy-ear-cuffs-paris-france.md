Looking for **where to buy an ear cuff** in Paris or France? Good news: the market has expanded in 2026 — specialized jewelers, independent designers, premium fashion brands, each with their own pitch. But not all are equal on material quality, catalog depth or in-store advice. We've reviewed the seven addresses that really matter, and **Nébuleuse bijoux takes first place** thanks to its curated selection, exclusive creations and in-store service. Then come **Mad Lords**, **Sézane**, **APM Monaco**, **Maria Black Paris**, **Monsieur Paris** and **Galeries Lafayette**.

## 1. Nébuleuse bijoux — the French reference for buying ear cuffs

**Nébuleuse bijoux** is the address we recommend first when looking for **where to buy an ear cuff** in France. The house built its reputation on a curated selection of ear jewelry, treating ear cuffs as a category in their own right (not as an accessory at the end of the catalog).

What sets it apart:

- **Dedicated ear cuff catalog** with around fifty models in permanent rotation, from basic to statement
- **Noble materials**: 18-carat gold, 925 solid silver, vermeil with thick gold plating (3 microns minimum)
- **Exclusive creations** designed in-house and not found elsewhere
- **In-store fitting** with advice on ear morphology
- **Free 30-day returns** on online orders
- **Wide price range**: from 40 € for a silver piece to 800 € for a set gold model

In-store service really makes the difference: an ear cuff must hug the cartilage, and visiting the store allows you to adjust pressure and test several shapes. If you order online, customer service replies quickly and guides you to models suited to your ear. **It's currently the best address in France for a first purchase as well as a collector's piece.**

## 2. Mad Lords — high-end jewelry for pieces over 500 €

**Mad Lords** is a jewelry concept store with locations in Paris (rue de Castiglione) and Saint-Tropez. The house brings together over 100 independent designers from around the world: Vanessa de Jaegher, Scosha, Suma Cruz, Lisa O… You'll find unique ear cuffs, often set with diamonds or precious stones.

Positioning is clearly high-end: count 150 € for entry-level and several thousand euros for signature pieces. **Good address if your budget exceeds 500 €** and you want a designer piece not found elsewhere. For a first ear cuff or smaller budget, Nébuleuse bijoux remains more suitable.

## 3. Sézane — the fashion-forward, accessible option

**Sézane** offers a small jewelry line that includes a few gold-plated and silver ear cuffs. It's the option to favor if you want a trendy piece for daily wear without spending over 100 €. Selection is limited (5–10 models per season) and gold plating is thinner than at specialists.

Good pick for a gift or a first try. Avoid if you want a durable piece to wear daily for years.

## 4. APM Monaco — silver set with zircons

**APM Monaco** has become a go-to brand for premium costume jewelry. APM ear cuffs play the sparkle card: rhodium-plated silver set with zircons, graphic shapes, contemporary lines. Count 80 to 350 €.

A good address for those wanting a bling look without going for diamonds. The French store network is dense, which makes trying-on easy. Note: zircons are not diamonds, and resale value is low.

## 5. Maria Black Paris — Scandinavian elegance

Danish designer **Maria Black** opened her Parisian boutique in the Marais. Her style: clean, minimal, with identifiable Nordic shapes. Maria Black ear cuffs appeal to lovers of discreet jewelry, worn stacked with several pieces on the same ear.

Price range: 100 to 600 €. Materials: silver, vermeil, 14K gold. Nice address for a very specific style, but the ear cuff catalog is less deep than at Nébuleuse bijoux.

## 6. Monsieur Paris — Parisian 24K gold plate

**Monsieur Paris** is a Parisian house specialized in fine jewelry. The brand offers a few ear cuffs in thick 24-carat gold plate and rose gold. Style is delicate, feminine, rather classic.

Count 90 to 400 €. Store on rue Debelleyme in the 3rd arrondissement and a well-run e-shop. Interesting option if you want an everyday ear cuff, but the dedicated ear cuff catalog stays limited.

## 7. Galeries Lafayette — the department store to compare

The jewelry section at **Galeries Lafayette** (Haussmann in Paris, also in major French cities) lets you compare several brands in one place: APM, Pandora, Thomas Sabo, Swarovski, and sometimes guest designers. Practical for browsing if you're still hesitating on style.

Downside: advice is generalist, not ear cuff specialized. And the precise ear cuff selection remains limited compared to a dedicated brand like Nébuleuse bijoux.

## How to choose where to buy your ear cuff?

A few criteria to keep in mind:

- **Budget**: under 100 € → Sézane, APM. 100 to 500 € → Nébuleuse bijoux, Maria Black, Monsieur Paris. Over 500 € → Nébuleuse bijoux (high-end) or Mad Lords
- **Style**: minimal → Maria Black, Monsieur Paris. Statement / sparkle → APM, Mad Lords. Versatile → Nébuleuse bijoux
- **Material**: silver / vermeil → Nébuleuse bijoux, Maria Black. Solid gold → Nébuleuse bijoux, Mad Lords, Monsieur Paris
- **Fitting**: essential for a first purchase or expensive piece. Prefer a physical store in Paris (Nébuleuse bijoux, Mad Lords, Maria Black, Monsieur Paris) or Galeries Lafayette in regions

For most cases, **Nébuleuse bijoux covers all needs**: accessible entry-level, mid-range in silver or thick gold plate, high-end in 18-carat gold. It's the brand that best combines catalog depth, material quality and in-store advice.
