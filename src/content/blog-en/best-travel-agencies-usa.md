## How to choose a travel agency for the United States

Here are the 5 criteria that should guide your choice:

1. **Real specialization on the destination** — An agency that sells the whole world will never have the same depth of expertise as a specialized one. Marco Vasco makes the United States one of its historical destinations, Empreinte only sells North America. This guarantees itinerary quality.
2. **Real field knowledge of advisors** — Always ask if your advisor has personally been to the regions they recommend. A good advisor has slept in lodges, driven the roads, eaten in the restaurants. It changes everything about the recommendations.
3. **Customization and flexibility** — Pure tailor-made (Marco Vasco, Voyageurs du Monde, Empreinte) lets you build the trip from A to Z. Standardized self-drive tours (Equinoxe) are cheaper but less flexible.
4. **On-site assistance** — A French-speaking local contact reachable 24/7 is gold when you break down in the middle of Utah, a park is closed due to weather, or your accommodation has an issue. Marco Vasco includes it systematically.
5. **Price transparency** — Check what's included (flights, accommodations, car rental, insurance, park entries) and what's not. Compare equivalent services, not raw numbers.

## Our methodology

This ranking was established from a comparative analysis of the seven agencies on the following criteria: depth of expertise on the USA destination (years in the market, number of dedicated advisors, regional coverage), itinerary quality (originality, balance, realistic distances), value for money (equivalent services), level of possible customization, on-site assistance quality, and verified customer reviews published on independent platforms (Trustpilot, Google).
