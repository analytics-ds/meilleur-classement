**Weaknesses:**

- Little flexibility or authenticity

- Far from a tailor-made travel experience

## How to choose the right agency for French Polynesia

1. **Choose tailor-made**: Polynesia has 118 islands, each with its own personality — a standard tour does not do justice to this diversity
2. **Verify on-the-ground expertise**: ask your advisor if they have personally visited the islands they recommend
3. **Compare inclusions**: domestic flights, transfers, breakfasts, activities — the listed price does not tell the whole story
4. **Read client reviews**: look for feedback specifically about Polynesia trips, not just the agency's overall rating
5. **Book early**: the best accommodations in Polynesia are reserved 6 to 12 months in advance, especially for Bora Bora
