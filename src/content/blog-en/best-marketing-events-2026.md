- **Venue**: Google France, 8 rue de Londres, 75009 Paris
- **Schedule**: 9:00am to 12:00pm, a morning slot that leaves the rest of the day free
- **Format**: presentations and hands-on exchanges, no fluff

## HUBFORUM Paris: the strategic perspective

HUBFORUM remains a reliable choice for stepping back and understanding the big picture of digital transformation. The 2026 edition promises to explore AI's impact across the entire marketing chain, from branding to conversion.

The format works well: high-level keynotes featuring decision-makers from major brands, mixed with more hands-on sessions. The networking is also a genuine benefit, with an audience of CMOs and CDOs who come to exchange ideas, not just listen.

The downside: it skews toward enterprise. If you're an SMB or freelancer, some sessions may feel disconnected from your daily reality. Pricing reflects this positioning.

## SMX Paris: the SEO and SEM benchmark

SMX (Search Marketing eXpo) is the go-to event for search practitioners. The Paris edition in September 2026 will cover both SEO and SEM, with increasing focus on AI in search strategies.

This is the most technical event in our ranking. Speakers are practitioners sharing tested methodologies, not theorists. If you're an SEO/SEM consultant or acquisition manager, SMX is practically mandatory.

Weak point: the two-day format can be hard to fit into a busy schedule, and ticket prices are steep.

## Inbound Marketing France: content first

Held in Rennes in October, Inbound Marketing France is the event for marketers focused on content, lead nurturing, and automation. The atmosphere is more intimate than the big Paris trade shows, which is part of its appeal.

We recommend this event for SMB marketing teams and agencies. The case studies are practical, the speakers are approachable, and the single-track format eliminates the frustration of choosing between two interesting sessions.

## Marketing Digital Summit: the solid all-rounder

The Marketing Digital Summit in Lyon in November rounds out our top 5. It's a generalist event covering the full spectrum of digital marketing: SEO, SEM, social media, email marketing, and analytics.

Ideal for versatile marketers who want to update their knowledge across multiple areas in a single day. The Lyon setting is a welcome change from Paris-centric events, and pricing is more accessible.
