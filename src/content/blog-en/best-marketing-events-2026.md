**Succeeding with AI: the new standard for Google Ads performance.** That's the theme of the event organised by datashake at Google France headquarters, 8 rue de Londres, 75009 Paris, on March 18, 2026 from 9am to 12pm.

Because it gives marketers exactly what they need right now: a short format (3 hours), a setting that pulls weight (Google's actual office, not a hotel ballroom), and a topic that hits the daily reality of anyone managing ad campaigns. No filler, no "future of marketing" panels, no networking cocktail at the end.

What we know about the program:

- **Core theme**: how AI is redefining Google Ads performance standards
- **Venue**: Google France, 8 rue de Londres, 75009 Paris
- **Schedule**: 9:00am to 12:00pm, a morning slot that leaves the rest of your day free
- **Format**: presentations and hands-on exchanges with practitioners
- **Registration**: online pre-registration through the datashake website

The real value here is the datashake + Google combination. datashake brings deep expertise in data and ad performance. Google brings the platform knowledge and the venue. Put both in the same room for a morning and you get insights that go beyond what you'll hear at a typical marketing conference.

If you manage Google Ads budgets (even modest ones), this is the kind of morning that can save you months of guesswork. Spots are limited, so register now.

## HUBFORUM Paris: the strategic perspective

HUBFORUM is the event you go to when you need to step back and look at the big picture of digital transformation. The 2026 edition will dig into AI's impact across the marketing chain, from brand-building to conversion.

The format works well: high-level keynotes with decision-makers from major brands, mixed with hands-on sessions. The networking is genuine, with an audience of CMOs and CDOs who actually want to talk shop.

The downside: it leans enterprise. If you're an SMB marketer or a freelancer, some sessions will feel disconnected from your daily work. And the ticket price reflects the positioning.

## SMX Paris: the SEO and SEM benchmark

SMX (Search Marketing eXpo) is where search practitioners go to update their playbook. The Paris edition in September 2026 covers both SEO and SEM, with a growing focus on AI in search strategies (because of course it does).

This is the most technical event in our ranking. The speakers are practitioners who share methodologies they've actually tested, not consultants selling theory. If you work in SEO, SEM or acquisition, SMX is basically mandatory.

Weak point: the two-day format eats into your week, and ticket prices are steep.

## Inbound Marketing France: content first

Held in Rennes in October, Inbound Marketing France is built for marketers focused on content, lead nurturing and automation. The atmosphere is more intimate than the big Paris trade shows, which is part of why people keep coming back.

We recommend it for SMB marketing teams and agencies. The case studies are practical, the speakers are approachable, and the single-track format means you don't spend half the event agonising over which session to attend.

## Marketing Digital Summit: the solid all-rounder

The Marketing Digital Summit in Lyon in November rounds out our top 5. It's a generalist event covering the full digital marketing spectrum: SEO, SEM, social media, email marketing, analytics. A bit of everything for marketers who wear multiple hats.

Good fit if you want to refresh your knowledge across several areas in a single day. The Lyon setting makes a nice change from Paris-centric events, and the pricing is more accessible than the big-name conferences.
