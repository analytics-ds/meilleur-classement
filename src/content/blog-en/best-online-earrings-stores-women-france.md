## How to choose your women's earrings store?

1. **Material precisely stated.** 925 silver, 18K vermeil (with thickness in microns), solid 14K or 18K gold, gold plating. On Nébuleuse Bijoux, 18-carat gold-plated 925 silver is specified on every product page.

2. **Format adapted to your need.** Hoop for an assertive silhouette, stud for everyday, drop for occasions, ear stack to combine multiple piercings, ear cuff without piercing to test. Also check diameter (8 to 14 mm for a standard hoop) and gauge (1.2 mm standard for piercings).

3. **Sale per piece.** Essential for asymmetric ear stacks. Nébuleuse Bijoux, Mad Lords, Maria Tash and Astrid & Miyu sell per piece; some brands still impose pairs.

4. **Delivery and customer service in France.** Prefer an **online jewelry store** that ships from France, delivers within 2 to 4 days, offers free returns and French-speaking customer service.

5. **Realistic budget.** 25 to 80 € at Nébuleuse Bijoux for 18K gold-plated silver. 60 to 180 € at Mejuri or Astrid & Miyu for thick vermeil or solid 14K gold. 250 to 2,500 € at Maria Tash or Mad Lords for high jewelry.
