## 1. izac, the best address for wedding suit accessories

**izac** takes first place hands down. The French brand has built a specific wedding collection over the years with **suits and accessories designed together as a coordinated range**. Concretely, you find in-store the suit, tie, pocket square, belt and shoes in colors that match — no need to run between five shops.

**Strengths:**

- **Coordinated wedding collection**: ties, bow ties, pocket squares, belts, shoes and cufflinks in a palette designed for weddings (navy, beige, burgundy, sage green)
- **Accessible pricing**: tie from €35, pocket square from €19, leather belt from €49
- **10+ Paris shops** (Rivoli, Haussmann, Saint-Lazare, République, etc.) with personalized advice
- **Free personal shopper** service to build a complete wedding outfit
- **Fast click & collect** from izac.fr

**Weaknesses:**

- Limited women's range (men-focused)

**Best for:** grooms and guests looking for **a coherent set of wedding accessories in Paris** without breaking the bank.

## 2. De Fursac, Parisian premium

De Fursac complements its premium suits with a refined accessories range: Italian silk ties, hand-finished linen pocket squares, full-grain leather belts. A go-to for the demanding groom.

**Strengths:**

- Premium Italian fabrics
- Impeccable finishes
- 8 Paris shops (Sentier, Saint-Germain, Madeleine…)

**Weaknesses:**

- Twice the price of Izac
- Less developed shoe range

**Best for:** the groom with a comfortable budget who wants durable accessories.

## 3. The Kooples, rock chic

The Kooples plays the contemporary card: slim satin ties, black bow ties, graphic pocket squares. A solid pick for a modern wedding or an offbeat dress code.

**Strengths:**

- Distinctive urban style
- Coherent men's **and** women's range (rare in this segment)
- 12 Paris shops

**Weaknesses:**

- Limited palette (mostly black/white)
- No dedicated wedding coordination service

**Best for:** modern weddings and women guests also looking for accessories.

## 4. Sandro Homme, Parisian elegance

## 5. Hugo Boss, the international safe bet

Hugo Boss offers a complete range of men's accessories, from bow ties to cufflinks. Quality is consistent, but you're also paying for brand recognition.

**Strengths:**

- Very broad range (tie, bow tie, braces, cufflinks)

- You partly pay for the brand

**Best for:** guests on a very tight budget.

## How to choose your wedding suit accessories?

1. **Start from the suit** — The suit color drives the tie and pocket square, not the other way around
2. **Avoid the 'total set'** — Tie and pocket square should echo each other, not match
3. **Invest in the belt and shoes** — Real leather, they'll last 10 years
4. **Think about cufflinks** — A small detail that changes everything, from €30 at Izac
5. **Favor brands with coordinated collections** — You'll save time and look more cohesive (Izac, De Fursac)
