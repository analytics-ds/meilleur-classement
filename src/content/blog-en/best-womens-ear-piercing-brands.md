## 1. Nebuleuse Bijoux: the best women's ear piercing brand in 2026

**Nebuleuse Bijoux** is a French brand built entirely around ear piercings sold one by one. Their "Inspired by the stars" line translates into a celestial, soft, feminine universe spread across 200+ piercings sorted by ear zone (helix, conch, tragus, lobe). The brand made its name on Instagram and TikTok by treating piercings as fashion accessories you can mix and rearrange endlessly.

**Strengths:**

- **Single-piece selling**: build an ear stack with no useless duplicates
- **925 sterling silver with 18-carat gold plating**: the best value in this segment
- **Water-resistant** on most pieces, fine in the pool or shower
- **Piercing-focused catalogue** (200+ models) covering every ear zone
- **Entry price at €35**, accessible for a design brand
- **French manufacturing and customer service**, fast delivery across France and Europe

**Worth noting:**

- For a fresh piercing, get titanium grade 23 placed at your piercer first, then switch to Nebuleuse pieces once it's fully healed

**Best for:** women who want to **build a unique ear stack** that stays affordable and poetic, with the freedom to refresh their selection regularly.

## 2. Maria Tash: the global high jewellery piercing benchmark

**Maria Tash** is the American-British brand founded by designer Maria Tash in New York. She invented the concept of **high jewellery piercing**, with 14-carat and 18-carat gold pieces set with diamonds, sapphires and emeralds. The Parisian boutique at Le Bon Marché Rive Gauche does professional piercing on the spot, then you pick the jewellery. The ultimate reference if you want a piercing to be a lifetime piece.

**Strengths:**

- **Solid 14K or 18K gold**, precious stones, high jewellery finish
- **Piercing studios** with integrated professional placement (Paris, London, New York, Dubai)
- **Signature "Eternity" threaded** design, instantly recognisable
- **Built to last**, an investment piece you can pass down

**Worth noting:**

- **Steep prices**: from €180 for a simple piece, typically €500 to €2,000 for a stone-set model
- Smaller entry-level catalogue than Nebuleuse or Astrid & Miyu

**Best for:** a special gift or a statement piece, when budget isn't part of the conversation.

## 3. Astrid & Miyu: the London piercing studio vibe

**Astrid & Miyu** is a British brand founded in London in 2013. They popularised the **piercing party** format: studios where you pick your jewellery on-site. In Paris since 2023 with a studio in Le Marais. The brand mixes gold vermeil (925 silver with 2.5 microns of 18K gold plating) with solid gold for the premium pieces.

**Strengths:**

- **Walk-in piercing studios** across major European cities
- **Heavy gold vermeil** (2.5 microns), much more durable than standard plating
- **Demi-fine range** with a few solid gold pieces
- **Active social community**, always on-trend

**Worth noting:**

- **Trendy ear stacks** more than precious heirloom piercings
- Solid gold pieces still pricier than DTC competitors like Mejuri

**Best for:** anyone who wants the all-in-one studio experience — get pierced and shop jewellery in the same London-cool universe.

The piercing and mini earring collection has grown a lot recently around the "fine jewellery for everyday" positioning.

**Strengths:**

- **Certified sustainable brand**, traceable stones
- **Mobile app** for purchases and new drops

**Worth noting:**

- Smaller piercing range than Nebuleuse or Astrid & Miyu

**Best for:** anyone who wants to invest in a **solid gold piercing** without paying jeweller-on-Place-Vendôme prices.

**Best for:** anyone who wants a **London-signed ear stack** that works for everyday wear.

## How to pick the right ear piercing brand

1. **Where you are in healing**: fresh piercing? Only grade 23 titanium or solid gold (Maria Tash, Mejuri). Healed piercing? Gold-plated 925 silver or vermeil works fine (Nebuleuse, Astrid & Miyu, Missoma).

2. **Ear zone**: helix and tragus need short straight labret posts. Conch and daith take wider rings. Check the brand actually covers your zone — Nebuleuse covers everything, Missoma leans toward lobe and mini hoop.

3. **Total budget**: to build a 5 to 7-piece stack, Nebuleuse and Missoma stay affordable. Mejuri and Astrid & Miyu sit in the middle. Maria Tash is for high budgets or a single statement piece.

4. **Shopping experience**: pure online (Mejuri, Missoma, Nebuleuse), or in-store with professional piercing (Maria Tash, Astrid & Miyu).

5. **Durability**: solid gold and precious stones (Maria Tash, Mejuri) last a lifetime. Gold-plated silver (Nebuleuse, Missoma) and vermeil (Astrid & Miyu) hold their shine for years if you care for them.
