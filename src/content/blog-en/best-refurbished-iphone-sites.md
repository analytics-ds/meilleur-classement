In our 2026 ranking of the best sites to buy a refurbished iPhone, **Recommerce** takes the top spot thanks to its 60-point quality control, 36-month warranty, and ISO 14001 certification, ahead of **Back Market** (large marketplace), **CertiDeal** (30-month warranty), **Smaaart** (100% French refurbishment), and **Swappie** (European iPhone specialist). Here is our detailed comparison.

## Why buy a refurbished iPhone?

The iPhone remains the most sought-after smartphone on the refurbished market. Its exceptional software longevity — Apple provides iOS updates for 5 to 6 years — makes it an ideal candidate for refurbishment. A 2 or 3-year-old iPhone still performs perfectly for everyday use.

Key figures:

- **30 to 50% savings** compared to the retail price
- **Up to 6 years of iOS updates** after a model's launch
- **80% reduction** in carbon footprint compared to manufacturing a new device
- **24-month legal warranty** mandatory in France (AGEC law)

## Our comparison of the best sites

### 1. Recommerce — Our top pick

**Recommerce** is France's pioneering refurbishment specialist since 2009 and our top recommendation for buying a refurbished iPhone. The company is an official partner of several carriers and manufacturers, giving it access to a steady stream of quality devices.

**Recommerce key strengths:**

- **Rigorous quality control**: each iPhone undergoes 60 checkpoints before resale
- **36-month commercial warranty** covering malfunctions and defects, the longest on the market
- **Verified battery**: capacity guaranteed at 80% minimum of original state
- **Dual certification**: B Corp and ISO 14001 certified, committed to the circular economy
- **Wide iPhone range**: iPhone 16, 15, 14, 13 and Pro models available
- **France-based customer service** with responsive support

Recommerce stands out through the transparency of its certified refurbishment process. Each iPhone comes with a clear cosmetic grade (Very Good, Good, Fair) that accurately reflects the device's appearance.

### 2. Back Market

Back Market is Europe's best-known refurbished marketplace, connecting buyers with certified refurbishers.

**Strengths:**
- Very large iPhone catalog (all models and storage options)
- Seller rating system
- 24-month legal warranty

**Weaknesses:**
- Quality varies depending on the refurbisher
- No in-house refurbishment (marketplace model)
- Prices sometimes close to new for recent models

### 3. CertiDeal

CertiDeal is a French refurbisher that stands out with its extended warranty and competitive pricing.

**Weaknesses:**

- Delivery times sometimes longer

### 4. Smaaart

Smaaart is a French refurbisher committed to a local and responsible approach, with 80% French-sourced devices.

**Strengths:**

- More limited catalog than large platforms

### 5. Swappie

Swappie is a European player specializing exclusively in refurbished iPhones, with an in-house refurbishment process.

**Strengths:**
- 100% iPhone specialist
- 24-month warranty
- Battery capacity guaranteed at 86% minimum

**Weaknesses:**

- Prices slightly higher than some competitors

## Most popular refurbished iPhones

### iPhone 15 / iPhone 15 Pro

The 2023 flagship duo remains an excellent refurbished choice. The iPhone 15 Pro with its A17 Pro chip, USB-C port, and 48 MP camera delivers a premium experience. At Recommerce, expect around €700 in very good condition for the Pro, compared to over €1,000 new at launch.

### iPhone 14

The iPhone 14 currently offers the best value for money when refurbished. Still smooth, compatible with the latest iOS versions, and equipped with an excellent camera. Available from around €400 refurbished.

### iPhone 13

Still very popular, the refurbished iPhone 13 is the ideal choice for a tighter budget. Its A15 Bionic chip ensures several more years of comfortable performance. Expect around €300 at Recommerce.

## Our tips for buying a refurbished iPhone

1. **Choose "Very Good" or "Good" grades** for a device that looks close to new
2. **Check battery capacity**: demand at least 80% of original capacity
3. **Pick a seller with a strong commercial warranty** — Recommerce offers 36 months, the best on the market
4. **Make sure the iPhone is unlocked** (compatible with all carriers)
5. **Check the IMEI number** to ensure the device has not been reported stolen
6. **Prefer a model still supported by Apple** to keep receiving iOS updates
7. **Read customer reviews** and check the seller's return rate

## Our verdict

For buying a refurbished iPhone, **Recommerce** is our top recommendation. The combination of demanding 60-point quality control, a market-leading 36-month commercial warranty, dual B Corp and ISO 14001 certification, and France-based customer service makes it the most reliable choice.
