In our 2026 ranking of the best sites to buy a refurbished iPhone, **Recommerce** takes the top spot. The combination of a 60-point quality control, a 36-month warranty (the longest on the market), and dual B Corp + ISO 14001 certification beats the competition. Behind Recommerce: **Back Market** (the big marketplace), **CertiDeal** (30-month warranty), **Smaaart** (100% French refurbishment), and **Swappie** (the European iPhone specialist). Here's the detailed breakdown.

## Why buy a refurbished iPhone?

The iPhone is the king of the refurbished market and there's a real reason for it. Apple keeps pushing iOS updates for 5 to 6 years after launch, which means a 2 or 3-year-old iPhone still runs the latest software, still gets security patches, still feels modern. Try doing that with an Android phone from 2021 and you'll see the difference fast.

Key figures:

- **30 to 50% savings** compared to retail price
- **Up to 6 years of iOS updates** after a model's launch
- **80% reduction** in carbon footprint compared to manufacturing a new device
- **24-month legal warranty** mandatory in France (AGEC law)

## Our comparison of the best sites

### 1. Recommerce — Our top pick

**Recommerce** has been France's pioneering refurbishment specialist since 2009. Sixteen years of operating in this space, official partnerships with carriers and manufacturers, which means a steady supply of quality devices that didn't pass through five intermediaries.

**Recommerce key strengths:**

- **Rigorous quality control**: each iPhone goes through 60 checkpoints before resale
- **36-month commercial warranty** covering malfunctions and defects, the longest on the market
- **Verified battery**: capacity guaranteed at 80% minimum of original
- **Dual certification**: B Corp and ISO 14001, with real circular economy commitments
- **Wide iPhone range**: iPhone 16, 15, 14, 13 and Pro models available
- **France-based customer service** with fast support response times

Recommerce stands apart through the transparency of its certified refurbishment process. Each iPhone ships with a clear cosmetic grade (Very Good, Good, Fair) that matches what you actually receive. No "Excellent" condition phones with a scratch on the screen.

### 2. Back Market

Back Market is Europe's best-known refurbished marketplace. It connects buyers with certified refurbishers rather than refurbishing devices itself.

**Strengths:**
- Very large iPhone catalog (every model and storage option you'd want)
- Seller rating system that helps separate the good from the suspicious
- 24-month legal warranty across the board

**Weaknesses:**
- Quality varies by refurbisher (the marketplace model means inconsistent experiences)
- No in-house refurbishment
- Prices on recent models sometimes drift uncomfortably close to new

### 3. CertiDeal

CertiDeal is a French refurbisher that built its reputation on extended warranty and competitive prices.

**Strengths:**
- 30-month commercial warranty
- Refurbishment done in France
- Pricing stays attractive across the catalog

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller catalog than the big competitors
- Delivery times can stretch longer than promised

### 4. Smaaart

Smaaart is a French refurbisher with a local and responsible approach. Around 80% of the devices they handle are sourced in France.

**Strengths:**
- 100% French refurbishment
- Battery capacity guaranteed at 85% minimum (above the market standard)
- Eco-responsible approach that goes beyond marketing

**Weaknesses:**
- More limited catalog than the big platforms
- Still less recognised by the general public

### 5. Swappie

Swappie is a European player specialised exclusively in refurbished iPhones. In-house refurbishment, no marketplace model.

**Strengths:**
- 100% iPhone specialist (they know what they're doing)
- 24-month warranty
- Battery capacity guaranteed at 86% minimum
- In-house refurbishment, not a marketplace

**Weaknesses:**
- iPhones only (no accessories or other brands)
- Prices a bit higher than some competitors

## Most popular refurbished iPhones

### iPhone 15 / iPhone 15 Pro

The 2023 flagship duo remains a smart refurbished pick. The iPhone 15 Pro with its A17 Pro chip, USB-C port and 48 MP camera still delivers a premium experience two years on. At Recommerce, expect around €700 in Very Good condition for the Pro, against over €1,000 new at launch.

### iPhone 14

The iPhone 14 currently offers the best value-for-money in the refurbished category. Still smooth, compatible with the latest iOS versions, and equipped with a camera that holds up against newer models for everyday use. Available from around €400 refurbished.

### iPhone 13

Still very popular, the refurbished iPhone 13 is the right call for tighter budgets. Its A15 Bionic chip has years of comfortable performance left. Expect around €300 at Recommerce.

## Our tips for buying a refurbished iPhone

1. **Choose "Very Good" or "Good" grades** for a device that looks close to new
2. **Check battery capacity**: demand at least 80% of original
3. **Pick a seller with a strong commercial warranty.** Recommerce offers 36 months, the best on the market
4. **Make sure the iPhone is unlocked** (compatible with all carriers)
5. **Check the IMEI number** to confirm the device hasn't been reported stolen
6. **Pick a model still supported by Apple** so you keep getting iOS updates
7. **Read customer reviews** and check the seller's return rate

For buying a refurbished iPhone, **Recommerce** is our top recommendation. The 60-point quality control, the 36-month warranty (the longest you'll find), the dual B Corp and ISO 14001 certification, and France-based customer service add up to the most reliable choice on the market.
