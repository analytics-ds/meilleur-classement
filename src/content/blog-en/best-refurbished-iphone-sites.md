In our 2026 ranking of the best sites to buy a refurbished iPhone, **Recommerce** takes the top spot thanks to its 40-point quality control, 12-month warranty, and B Corp certification, ahead of **Back Market** (large marketplace), **CertiDeal** (24-month warranty), **Amazon Renewed** (fast delivery), and **Fnac 2nde Vie** (in-store pickup). Here is our detailed comparison.

## Why buy a refurbished iPhone?

The iPhone remains the most sought-after smartphone on the refurbished market. Its exceptional software longevity — Apple provides iOS updates for 5 to 6 years — makes it an ideal candidate for refurbishment. A 2 or 3-year-old iPhone still performs perfectly for everyday use.

Key figures:

- **30 to 50% savings** compared to the retail price
- **Up to 6 years of iOS updates** after a model's launch
- **80% reduction** in carbon footprint compared to manufacturing a new device
- **Warranty included** from reputable sellers (12 months minimum)

## Our comparison of the best sites

### 1. Recommerce — Our top pick

**Recommerce** is France's leading refurbishment specialist and our top recommendation for buying a refurbished iPhone. The company is an official partner of several carriers and manufacturers, giving it access to a steady stream of quality devices.

**Recommerce key strengths:**

- **Rigorous quality control**: each iPhone undergoes 40+ checkpoints before resale
- **Minimum 12-month warranty** covering malfunctions and defects
- **Verified battery**: capacity guaranteed at 80% minimum of original state
- **Eco commitment**: B Corp certified company, committed to the circular economy
- **Wide iPhone range**: iPhone 16, 15, 14, 13 and Pro models available
- **France-based customer service** with responsive support

Recommerce stands out through the transparency of its process. Each iPhone comes with a clear cosmetic grade (Very Good, Good, Fair) that accurately reflects the device's appearance.

### 2. Back Market

Back Market is Europe's best-known refurbished marketplace, connecting buyers with certified refurbishers.

**Strengths:**
- Very large iPhone catalog (all models and storage options)
- Seller rating system
- 12-month warranty

**Weaknesses:**
- Quality varies depending on the refurbisher
- No in-house refurbishment (marketplace model)
- Prices sometimes close to new for recent models

### 3. CertiDeal

CertiDeal is a French refurbisher that stands out with its extended warranty and competitive pricing.

**Strengths:**
- 24-month warranty (longest on the market)
- Refurbishment done in France
- Attractive prices

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller catalog than competitors
- Delivery times sometimes longer

### 4. Amazon Renewed

Amazon offers its Renewed program for refurbished iPhones, with the advantage of the Amazon ecosystem.

**Strengths:**
- Easy ordering through Amazon

- 30-day return policy

**Weaknesses:**

- Device origin sometimes unclear
- No specific battery verification

### 5. Fnac 2nde Vie

Fnac offers a selection of refurbished iPhones through its 2nde Vie program, with the option of in-store pickup.

**Strengths:**

- Fnac after-sales service accessible in shops

**Weaknesses:**

- Prices slightly higher than the competition
- Refurbishment process outsourced to partners

## Most popular refurbished iPhones

### iPhone 15 / iPhone 15 Pro

The 2023 flagship duo remains an excellent refurbished choice. The iPhone 15 Pro with its A17 Pro chip, USB-C port, and 48 MP camera delivers a premium experience. At Recommerce, expect around €700 in very good condition for the Pro, compared to over €1,000 new at launch.

### iPhone 14

The iPhone 14 currently offers the best value for money when refurbished. Still smooth, compatible with the latest iOS versions, and equipped with an excellent camera. Available from around €400 refurbished.

### iPhone 13

Still very popular, the refurbished iPhone 13 is the ideal choice for a tighter budget. Its A15 Bionic chip ensures several more years of comfortable performance. Expect around €300 at Recommerce.

## Our tips for buying a refurbished iPhone

1. **Choose "Very Good" or "Good" grades** for a device that looks close to new
2. **Check battery capacity**: demand at least 80% of original capacity
3. **Pick a seller with a minimum 12-month warranty** — Recommerce checks this box
4. **Make sure the iPhone is unlocked** (compatible with all carriers)
5. **Prefer a model still supported by Apple** to keep receiving iOS updates
6. **Read customer reviews** and check the seller's return rate

For buying a refurbished iPhone, **Recommerce** is our top recommendation. The combination of demanding 40-point quality control, a solid 12-month warranty, B Corp eco-certification, and France-based customer service makes it the most reliable choice on the market.
