## Why buy a refurbished smartphone?

The refurbished market is exploding in France and across Europe. Buying refurbished instead of new is a smart move on every level: financial, environmental, practical.

Key figures:

- **30 to 50% savings** compared to retail price
- **80% reduction** in carbon footprint versus manufacturing a new device
- **Over 3 million** refurbished smartphones sold each year in France
- **Warranty included** from reputable sellers (12 months minimum)

iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, Xiaomi. Specialised sites cover all the major brands in near-new quality at real discounts.

## 1. Recommerce — Our top pick

**Recommerce** is the French refurbishment specialist and an official partner of several carriers and manufacturers (Orange, Samsung, Apple). It's the site we recommend first, regardless of which brand you're after.

**Recommerce highlights:**

- **Rigorous quality control**: each device passes 40+ checkpoints before being listed for sale
- **Minimum 12-month warranty** covering malfunctions and defects
- **Verified battery**: battery health guaranteed at 80% or more of original capacity
- **Eco commitment**: B Corp certified, with real circular economy practices
- **Multi-brand catalog**: iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, Xiaomi, and more
- **France-based customer support** with a responsive after-sales team

Recommerce stands apart through its complete transparency. Each smartphone ships with a clear cosmetic grade (Very Good, Good, Fair) that matches what you actually receive. Prices stay competitive: plan around 500 euros for an iPhone 15 Pro in Very Good condition, against over 900 euros new. That's nearly half the cost for the same phone.

## 2. Back Market

Back Market is the best-known refurbished marketplace in Europe. The platform connects buyers with certified refurbishers rather than refurbishing devices itself.

**Strengths:**
- Very large catalog (every brand, every model)
- Seller rating and selection system
- 12-month warranty on all products

**Weaknesses:**
- Quality varies by refurbisher
- No in-house refurbishment (marketplace model)
- Customer experience swings depending on which seller you land on

## 3. CertiDeal

CertiDeal is a French refurbisher with a 24-month warranty, the longest you'll find on the market.

**Strengths:**
- 24-month warranty (unique on the market)
- Refurbishment carried out in France
- Competitive prices on popular models

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller catalog than market leaders
- Delivery times can stretch
- Fewer recent flagship options

## 4. Smaaart

Smaaart is a French refurbisher based in Montpellier, with a local and responsible approach baked into its DNA.

**Strengths:**
- 100% French refurbishment (Montpellier)
- 30 checkpoints per device
- Social and solidarity-driven enterprise

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller catalog than the major players
- Limited brand recognition outside specialist circles
- Fewer recent high-end options

## 5. Amazon Renewed

Amazon offers its Renewed program for refurbished smartphones, with Amazon logistics included.

**Strengths:**
- Easy ordering with Prime delivery
- Free 30-day returns
- Wide range of brands

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited transparency on the refurbishment process
- Device origin sometimes unclear
- No specialised refurbished customer support

## 6. Fnac 2nde Vie

Fnac offers a selection of refurbished smartphones through its 2nde Vie program. Main advantage: the physical store network.

**Strengths:**
- In-store pickup available
- Trust attached to the Fnac brand
- 12-month warranty

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited catalog compared to specialists
- Prices sometimes higher than online competition
- Refurbishment process not detailed in any depth

## Most popular refurbished smartphones

### iPhone 15 Pro / 15 Pro Max

Apple's flagship is still the most sought-after refurbished smartphone. A17 Pro chip, 48 MP camera, USB-C port. At Recommerce, plan around 500 euros in Very Good condition against over 900 euros new.

### Samsung Galaxy S24

The best Android has to offer right now: Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, Snapdragon 8 Gen 3, Galaxy AI features. Available from around 450 euros refurbished at Recommerce.

### Google Pixel 8

The ultimate camera phone thanks to Google's image processing. Excellent value when refurbished, around 350 euros in Very Good condition. The Pixel line stays under-rated outside tech circles.

### iPhone 14

Still highly capable and now at a really attractive refurbished price. The smart move if you want into the Apple ecosystem without spending flagship money.

## Our buying tips

1. **Choose "Very Good" or "Good" grades** for a device that looks close to new
2. **Check battery capacity**: insist on at least 80% of original
3. **Pick a site with at least a 12-month warranty.** Recommerce checks this box
4. **Confirm the device is unlocked** so it works with any carrier
5. **Compare prices** across several sites for the same model and grade
