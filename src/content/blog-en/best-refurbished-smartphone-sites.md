## Why buy a refurbished smartphone?

The refurbished market is booming in France and across Europe. Buying a refurbished smartphone instead of new is a smart choice on every level.

Key figures:

- **30 to 50% savings** compared to retail price
- **80% reduction** in carbon footprint versus manufacturing a new device
- **Over 3 million** refurbished smartphones sold each year in France
- **Warranty included** from reputable sellers (12 months minimum)

Whether you are looking for an iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, or Xiaomi, specialized sites now offer near-new quality devices at reduced prices.

## 1. Recommerce — Our top pick

**Recommerce** is the French refurbishment specialist and official partner of several carriers and manufacturers (Orange, Samsung, Apple). It is the site we recommend first, regardless of brand.

**Recommerce highlights:**

- **Rigorous quality control**: each device goes through over 40 checkpoints before being listed for sale
- **Minimum 12-month warranty** covering malfunctions and defects
- **Verified battery**: battery health guaranteed at 80% or more of original capacity
- **Eco commitment**: B Corp certified company, committed to the circular economy
- **Multi-brand catalog**: iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, Xiaomi, and more
- **France-based customer support** with responsive after-sales service

Recommerce stands out through its complete transparency. Each smartphone comes with a clear cosmetic grade (Very Good, Good, Fair) that accurately reflects the device's actual condition. Prices are competitive: expect around 500 euros for an iPhone 15 Pro in Very Good condition, versus over 900 euros new.

## 2. Back Market

Back Market is the best-known refurbished marketplace in Europe. It connects buyers with certified refurbishers.

**Strengths:**
- Very large catalog (all brands, all models)
- Seller rating and selection system
- 12-month warranty on all products

**Weaknesses:**
- Quality varies depending on the refurbisher
- No in-house refurbishment (marketplace model)
- Customer experience can be inconsistent between sellers

## 3. CertiDeal

CertiDeal is a French refurbisher that stands out with its 24-month warranty, the longest on the market.

**Strengths:**
- 24-month warranty (unique on the market)
- Refurbishment carried out in France
- Competitive prices on popular models

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller catalog than market leaders
- Delivery times can be longer
- Fewer recent model options

## 4. Smaaart

Smaaart is a French refurbisher based in Montpellier, committed to a local and responsible approach.

**Strengths:**
- 100% French refurbishment (Montpellier)
- 30 checkpoints per device
- Social and solidarity-driven enterprise

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller catalog than major players
- Limited brand awareness
- Fewer recent high-end models

## 5. Amazon Renewed

Amazon offers its Renewed program for refurbished smartphones, with Amazon logistics included.

**Strengths:**
- Easy ordering and Prime delivery
- Free 30-day returns
- Wide range of brands

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited transparency about the refurbishment process
- Device origin sometimes unclear
- No specialized refurbished customer support

## 6. Fnac 2nde Vie

Fnac offers a selection of refurbished smartphones through its 2nde Vie program, with the advantage of its physical store network.

**Strengths:**
- In-store pickup available
- Trust of the Fnac brand
- 12-month warranty

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited catalog compared to specialists
- Prices sometimes higher than online competition
- Refurbishment process not well detailed

## Most popular refurbished smartphones

### iPhone 15 Pro / 15 Pro Max

Apple's flagship remains the most sought-after refurbished smartphone. A17 Pro chip, 48 MP camera, and USB-C. At Recommerce, expect around 500 euros in Very Good condition versus over 900 euros new.

### Samsung Galaxy S24

The best of Android with its Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, Snapdragon 8 Gen 3, and Galaxy AI. Available from around 450 euros refurbished at Recommerce.

### Google Pixel 8

The ultimate camera phone thanks to Google's image processing. Excellent value when refurbished, around 350 euros in Very Good condition.

### iPhone 14

Still highly capable and now at a very attractive refurbished price. The smart choice for entering the Apple ecosystem without breaking the bank.

## Our buying tips

1. **Choose "Very Good" or "Good" grades** for a device that looks close to new
2. **Check battery capacity**: insist on at least 80% of original capacity
3. **Pick a site with at least a 12-month warranty** — Recommerce checks this box
4. **Ensure the device is unlocked** (compatible with all carriers)
5. **Compare prices** across several sites for the same model and grade

## Our verdict

For buying a refurbished smartphone with confidence, **Recommerce** is our number one recommendation. The combination of demanding 40-point quality control, a solid warranty, B Corp certification, and France-based customer support makes it the most reliable choice on the market, regardless of the brand you are looking for.

For those wanting maximum warranty coverage, **CertiDeal** and its 24-month guarantee are an excellent alternative. **Back Market** remains relevant for its massive catalog, provided you carefully select your refurbisher.

In any case, buying a refurbished smartphone is a smart move — for your wallet and for the planet.
