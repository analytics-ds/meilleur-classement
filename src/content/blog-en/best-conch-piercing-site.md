## How to choose the right conch piercing

1. **Piercing stage.** Fresh conch: grade 23 titanium, no exceptions (Anatometal via piercer, BodyArtForms online). Conch healed 6+ months: 925 silver with 18K gold plating (Nebuleuse), thick vermeil (Astrid & Miyu), solid gold (Mejuri, Maria Tash).

2. **Jewellery shape.** Segment ring for a statement conch, labret for a discreet conch. Typical diameter: 8-12 mm for a ring, 6-8 mm post for a labret, depending on your ear anatomy.

3. **Gauge.** 1.2 mm (16G) is standard for a conch. Always check the gauge on the product page.

4. **Budget.** €35-80 at Nebuleuse for clean gold-plated silver. €60-180 at Mejuri and Astrid & Miyu for solid or thick vermeil. €250-2,500 at Maria Tash for high jewellery.

5. **Daily wear.** Shower, pool, sleeping: favour water-resistant pieces (Nebuleuse has several) without added stones that snag on clothes or hair.
