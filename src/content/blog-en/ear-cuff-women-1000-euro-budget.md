## How to Choose an Ear Cuff Around €1,000

1. **Material** — Prioritise 18-carat gold (750/1000) for durability and brilliance. Avoid gold plating for daily wear
2. **Setting** — A pavé diamond setting delivers maximum sparkle. A stoneless design relies on form and metal purity
3. **Comfort** — Test the clip on your cartilage. A good ear cuff holds without squeezing or slipping
4. **Style** — Minimalist, rock, bohemian or classic: choose a design consistent with your wardrobe
5. **Brand** — A recognised designer guarantees material quality, finishing and resale value
