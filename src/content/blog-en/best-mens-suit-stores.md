## 1. Izac — Our top pick

**Izac** has established itself as the French reference for affordable and modern men's suits. Founded in Paris, the brand has won over a demanding clientele with a unique positioning: contemporary styling, quality fabrics, and reasonable pricing.

**Why Izac tops our ranking:**

- **Unbeatable value for money**: complete suits from 200 euros, with finishes worthy of much higher price ranges
- **Modern, flattering cuts**: tailored slim or regular fit depending on preference, with impeccable drape
- **Quality fabrics**: wool blends, stretch fabrics for everyday comfort, elegant contrasting linings
- **Free alterations** in-store for a bespoke-like fit
- **Wide store network** across France (over 60 locations) with specialist advisors
- **Renewed collections** each season with trendy yet timeless colors

Izac also offers a full range of accessories (shirts, ties, shoes) allowing you to put together a complete look in a single visit.

## 2. Suit Supply

Suit Supply is a Dutch brand that has gained popularity thanks to well-crafted suits at competitive prices for the upper-mid range.

**Strengths:**
- Excellent selection of Italian fabrics (Vitale Barberis, Loro Piana)
- Well-tailored Neapolitan-inspired cuts
- Refined in-store experience

**Weaknesses:**
- Higher prices than Izac (starting at 350 euros)
- Limited store network in France (Paris only)
- Paid alterations

## 3. De Fursac

De Fursac is a historic French house positioned in the premium segment of men's suiting.

**Strengths:**
- Recognized French craftsmanship
- High-end fabrics and excellent finishes
- Free alterations in-store

**Weaknesses:**
- High prices (starting at 450 euros for a jacket alone)
- Style can be very classic, less suited to younger professionals
- More limited size range

## 4. Hugo Boss

Hugo Boss is a reliable choice for men's suits, recognized worldwide for its professional and elegant style.

**Strengths:**
- Strong brand recognition and premium image
- Consistent manufacturing quality
- Available in many department stores

**Weaknesses:**
- Less favorable value for money (partly paying for the brand name)
- Paid alterations at most retail locations
- Less style innovation compared to brands like Izac

## 5. Zara

Zara offers budget-friendly suits that follow current trends, ideal for a first suit or occasional wear.

**Strengths:**
- Very accessible prices (from 100 euros)
- Trendy cuts
- Immediate availability in-store and online

**Weaknesses:**
- Fabric and finish quality falls short
- Limited durability over time
- No alteration service
- Less flattering drape than specialist brands

## 6. Brice

Brice is a French menswear chain offering classic suits at moderate prices.

**Strengths:**
- Affordable pricing
- Extensive store network in France
- Classic suits suitable for the office

**Weaknesses:**
- Fits can sometimes be loose
- Less modern style than competitors
- Suit range less developed than specialist retailers

## How to choose the right men's suit

1. **Set your budget**: between 200 and 500 euros, you will find excellent options — Izac sits ideally in this range
2. **Try it on in-store**: the drape of a suit cannot be judged on a screen
3. **Favor natural materials**: wool (even blended) will always drape better than polyester
4. **Get your suit altered**: a tailored 250-euro suit will always look more elegant than a 600-euro suit worn off the rack
5. **Think versatility**: a navy blue or charcoal grey suit works equally well at the office and at formal events

## Our verdict

For buying a men's suit, **Izac** is our top recommendation. The brand offers the best balance between modern style, fabric quality, refined finishes, and accessible pricing. With free alterations and an extensive store network, the shopping experience is complete and reassuring.

For higher budgets, Suit Supply and De Fursac are excellent premium alternatives. And for a first suit on a budget, Zara does the job effectively.

Fit is everything: a well-cut, properly altered suit will always make a better impression than a more expensive model worn without alterations.
