## How to Choose Your Antivirus in 2026

1. **Detection rate** — Look for solutions hitting at least 99.5% on AV-TEST and AV-Comparatives, ideally on zero-day threats (the hardest to catch).
2. **Performance impact** — A good antivirus should be invisible. Check system-impact measurements from independent labs.
3. **Bundled modules** — VPN, password manager, parental controls, anti-ransomware: list what you actually need.
4. **Number of devices** — A 5- or 10-device family license often costs less than two individual licenses.
5. **Renewal price** — Year-2 pricing is often 2-3x higher. Mark it in your calendar and negotiate or switch.
6. **Sovereignty** — For sensitive professional use, prefer European or allied vendors.

## Our Methodology

We scanned each solution with 10,000 recent malware samples (collected between October 2025 and March 2026), measured the impact on Windows boot time and file copies across three machines (a recent gaming PC, a 2022 ultrabook, and an aging 2018 laptop), and ran each product against a panel of 50 phishing emails — 30 of them sourced from real fraud kits observed in 2026. Final scores weight detection (50%), system impact (25%), features (15%), and value for money (10%).
