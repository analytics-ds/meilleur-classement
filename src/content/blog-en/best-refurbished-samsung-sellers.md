In our 2026 ranking of the best refurbished Samsung sellers, **Recommerce** takes the top spot. The 40-point quality control and 12-month warranty give it the edge, ahead of **Back Market** (large catalog), **Amazon Renewed** (fast delivery via Prime), and **CertiDeal** (24-month warranty). Here's the full analysis to help you pick the most reliable seller.

## Why choose a refurbished Samsung?

Samsung dominates the Android market with smartphones that hit hard on display quality, camera performance and long-term software support. Buying refurbished lets you get that quality at a real discount while cutting your environmental footprint by a serious margin.

Key figures:

- **30 to 50% savings** compared to retail price
- **80% reduction** in carbon footprint versus manufacturing a new device
- **Warranty included** from reputable sellers (12 months minimum)

## Our comparison of the best sellers

### 1. Recommerce — Our top pick

**Recommerce** is the French refurbishment specialist and an official partner of several carriers and manufacturers. It's the seller we recommend first for a refurbished Samsung, for several solid reasons.

**Recommerce highlights:**

- **Rigorous quality control**: each device passes 40+ checkpoints before being listed for sale
- **Minimum 12-month warranty** covering malfunctions and defects
- **Verified battery**: battery health guaranteed at 80% or more of original capacity
- **Eco commitment**: B Corp certified company with real circular economy practices
- **Wide Samsung range**: Galaxy S24, S23, S22, Galaxy A54, A34 and many more
- **France-based customer support** with a responsive after-sales service

Recommerce stands apart through its transparent refurbishment process. Each smartphone ships with a clear cosmetic grade (Very Good, Good, Fair) that matches the actual condition of the device when you open the box.

### 2. Back Market

Back Market is the best-known refurbished marketplace in Europe. The platform connects buyers with certified refurbishers rather than refurbishing devices itself.

**Strengths:**
- Very large catalog
- Seller rating system
- 12-month warranty across the board

**Weaknesses:**
- Quality varies by refurbisher
- No in-house refurbishment (marketplace model)
- Customer experience swings from great to frustrating depending on the seller

### 3. Amazon Renewed

Amazon offers its Renewed program for refurbished products, including Samsung Galaxy devices.

**Strengths:**
- Easy ordering through your existing Amazon account
- 30-day return policy
- Fast Prime delivery

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited transparency on the refurbishment process
- Device origin sometimes unclear
- Warranty limited to Amazon's standard policy

### 4. CertiDeal

CertiDeal is a French refurbisher offering Samsung smartphones at competitive prices.

**Strengths:**
- Attractive pricing
- Refurbishment done in France
- 24-month warranty (double the legal minimum)

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller catalog than competitors
- Delivery times can run longer than promised

## Most popular refurbished Samsung models

### Samsung Galaxy S24 / S24+

The 2024 flagship is the smart refurbished pick right now. Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor, Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, Galaxy AI features that aren't gimmicks. At Recommerce, plan around €500 in Very Good condition versus over €800 new. Same phone, €300 less.

### Samsung Galaxy S23

Still highly capable, the Galaxy S23 delivers great value when refurbished. The right choice if you want premium Samsung quality without the premium price tag, and you don't need the very latest AI features.

### Samsung Galaxy A54

For tighter budgets, the refurbished Galaxy A54 is a safe bet. Super AMOLED display, solid battery life, guaranteed updates for 4 years. The A54 punches well above its price class.

## Tips for buying a refurbished Samsung

1. **Choose "Very Good" or "Good" grades** for a device that looks close to new
2. **Check battery capacity**: insist on at least 80% of original
3. **Pick a seller with at least a 12-month warranty.** Recommerce checks this box (and most others)
4. **Confirm the device is unlocked** so it works with any carrier
5. **Read customer reviews** and check the seller's return rate
